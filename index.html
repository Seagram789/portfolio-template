The project charter provides your vision of the project as you understand it. Requirements Management Plan

XYZ Website Redesign


The requirements management plan will come in very handy for you as it provides the basis for properly documenting all the necessary information to efficiently manage all project requirements from the beginning to the end of the project. It includes requirements definition, requirements traceability, and delivery of all requirements. The requirements management plan is created early in the project lifecycle during the planning phase. The plan's ultimate goal is to lay the foundation for baselining all project requirements and then controlling any changes to the requirements baseline through the integrated change control process.

A critical component of a project's success is stakeholder management. Accordingly, it is necessary to identify the people and groups who could potentially affect or be affected by your project. In fact, A Guide to the Project Management Body of Knowledge (PMBOK) includes the stakeholder identification process in the Initiating process group. Before you should begin a project, you need to know who the project is supporting and who needs to be involved.
